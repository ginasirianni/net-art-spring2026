Getting Home

The street lights are on and are making the side streets brighter than ever.

I turn into my neighborhood.

Parking lot packed with cars, everything still looks the same as when it left.

I see my patio light on, which means my whole family is most likely home.

I park and sit in my car for a few minutes.

I grab all my bags, literally about six of them and close and lock my car.

As I am walkiing the snow and rain start to come down more.

Finally home at last.

But college is also home.

Start the drive again
