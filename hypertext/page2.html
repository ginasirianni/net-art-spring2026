Gas Station

Getting on to route 18 for my 45 minute drive home.

It is about 4:00pm so the sky is that pale winter caller before the sun starts to fully set.

My gas light comes on that I have about 10 miles to go.

I pull into a gas station on the right that I usually always stop at on my way home.

The man puts the gas pump into my car and the numbers start to go up.

I reach for my phone to text one of my roommates: "Miss you already."

It hasn't even been an hour since we each last saw eachother.

Getting back on the highway
