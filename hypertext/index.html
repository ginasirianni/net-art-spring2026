Leaving My College House For My Last Winter Break

The house is quiet, not totally empty yet, but quieter than usual.

It is offically senior year winter break, the last one.

I stand infront of the door of my college house ready to open and close it to leave.

The sane door I walked into sophomore year excited to be getting out of the dorms.

There is a mix of snow and rain on the steps outside of the front door.

Winter break is about to begin.

But this time it feels different than other times.

This isn't just leaving for a long weekend.

This is the last time I will be leaving for over a month long before it is time to offically lock the doors for good

I pull the front door shut and head to my car parked on the side of the house.

Heading to the gas station
